About Us

In the bustling province of Ilocos Norte, where tradition meets modernity, "U Chill" stands as a testament to the art of relaxation
and the celebration of flavor.With a proud legacy spanning branches, "U Chill Tea Cafe" has become synonymous with quality, community, and the
perfect blend of indulgence.

Finally, this is it, U Chillers!
We're now much ready to serve you, trough our new Website. You can now order U Chill’s products by clicking on the menu bar.
The fusion of your favorite tea shop and restaurant will open its doors for all of you! Bringing with you delicious drinks
and tasty snacks plus we will offer meals that will surely satisfy your cravings.

Founded with a vision to create a haven for tea enthusiasts, "U Chill" started as a humble endeavor to introduce Ilocos Norte to the diverse
and exquisite world of milk tea. What began as a single store soon blossomed into a network of 11 branches, weaving its presence into
the fabric of communities across the province.

Join the U Chill Experience:
Whether you're a tea connoisseur or a casual sipper, "U Chill" invites you to embark on a journey of flavors and serenity.
With 11 branches spread across Ilocos Norte, there's always a "U Chill" destination nearby
where you can escape, unwind, and savor the simple joy of a perfectly crafted cup of milk tea.
